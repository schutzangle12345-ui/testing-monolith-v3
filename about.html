Our Story

In most organizations, data is trapped. It's either locked behind expensive, complex Business Intelligence (BI) tools that require a degree to operate, or it's buried in spreadsheets that fail to communicate a clear narrative.

testing-monolith-v3 was born from a simple observation: non-technical users—marketers, product managers, and small business owners—were being left behind. They needed to build dashboards quickly, without coding, and without the steep learning curve of enterprise software.

Our platform bridges that gap by offering sensible defaults and professional design aesthetics out of the box. No more ugly charts, and no more paywalls for basic visualization needs.

Our Core Values

Simplicity First

Our Unique Model

How do we keep our core tools free? Unlike traditional software companies that rely solely on subscriptions, testing-monolith-v3 leverages a unique business model focused on value-added partnerships.

We generate revenue through affiliate relationships and lead fees within specialized industries—such as hospitality and restaurant platforms. By helping these venues visualize their reservation and growth data, we create a sustainable ecosystem that allows us to offer free visualization tools to students, teachers, and startup founders globally.
